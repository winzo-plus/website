Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Winzo Plus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Profile information (photos) if you upload them.

B. Use of Camera

Our App requests access to your device's camera to scan QR codes for redeeming offers and verifying partner transactions. Images scanned are processed locally or sent securely to our servers for verification and are not used for any other purpose.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App.
  • Verify user identity and facilitate secure transactions.
  • Manage your deals, wallet, and rewards.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or support.

3. Data Deletion (User Rights)

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data. You can do this by:

  • Navigating to Settings > Delete Account within the App.
  • Contacting us at support@winzo.plus.

Upon request, we will permanently delete your personal information from our active databases, subject to legal retention obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as analytics or cloud storage) that collect, monitor, and analyze information to improve App functionality. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

5. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@winzo.plus
